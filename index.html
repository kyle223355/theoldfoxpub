INFO ABOUT PUB AND OWNERS

The Old Fox pub originally dates back to post World War 1. It was a successful run up until the M1 motorway opened in 1959, separating Bricket Wood, Garston and other nearby towns, therefore weakening the trade and income that it was receiving. The pub remained open throughout the 1970sâ€“2000s, offering food, real ales, and community events. By the early 2000s, it was run under Punch Taverns, a large pub company that leased it to tenants.

In 2016, the pub shut for good. Almost 10 years later, it reopened on the 1/7/2025, taken over by Danny and Julie Harding, a local couple who wish to restore the pub and its community. The Old Fox offers refreshing beverages for everyone, pets included. Whether you're a regular or a first-timer, you're part of the family here.
